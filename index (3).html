About Flow

I am not just a designer.
I am a chief creative officer.

"I didn't know you were a designer."

For the longest time, design quietly played second fiddle in my creative journey. While I was busy writing, managing socials, and helping Web3 projects turn complex ideas into simple, powerful messages I was making designs in the background.

Everything shifted in 2025. What started as a supporting skill suddenly took center stage. The transition was messy and uncomfortable, but it forced me to finally own every aspect of my skillset.

Now I work as a Creative Brand Designer and Strategist, specialising in Web3, AI, and Fintech building visual identities, brand systems, and creative strategies for protocols, KOLs, and foundations at the intersection of infrastructure and culture.
